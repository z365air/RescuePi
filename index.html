Before You Proceed

Pi Core Team does not allow developers to ask for user mnemonics or secret keys for user safety. Although this app is safe to use, if you want to abide by these restrictions and be certain of the safety of your funds, do not use this app and close this screen now.

Pi Network is a Stellar fork and this app lets you connect to your wallet through Horizon, the native Stellar blockchain interface. This allows you to perform operations that are not available on the official Pi Wallet, such as sweeping, collecting, sending, scheduling, and more. Note that any wallet you use on Pi Mainnet must still be KYC verified.

RescuePi is designed for users who have lost discretion of their wallet keys and are at risk of attackers. You have a chance to recover your future unlocks with this tool. Our bet is to be faster and more precise than the attackers.

If you understand the risks and the possibilities of using this app, type "I understand" into the box below and click Continue.
